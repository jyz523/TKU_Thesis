Introduction

The Department of English at Tamkang University aims to develop students' foundational skills in English language and basic professional competencies, preparing them for careers in education, commerce, and industry. The Master's program emphasizes academic research skills, teaching, translation, and cultural studies, while the Doctoral program cultivates advanced professionals in English and teaching research for roles in literature and education. Established in 1950, the department initially provided non-degree English language and literature courses for junior and senior high school graduates, transitioning in 1965 to a Bachelor’s degree curriculum. Since then, it has played a significant role in Taiwan’s English education, fostering proactive, professional, and globally-minded specialists for various public and private institutions through a robust faculty and diverse, dynamic teaching activities.

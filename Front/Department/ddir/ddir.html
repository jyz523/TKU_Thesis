Introduction

Our department started to recruit students in 2015. We established Department of Diplomacy and International Relations (DDIR) under the background of constantly changing globalization. Due to the shortening distances between international members, countries keep in touch with each other more nowadays and derive various complicated international issues.


The professional expertise of global vision has turned into basic knowledge in domestic education. The professional expertise didn't fade out along with the disappearing boundary between countries but became more important in diplomacy. The impact of globalization also affects Taiwan from the whole country to domestic industries. Thus, cultivating experts in theoretical, practical, and foreign language abilities is basic to the future development of the country. The establishment purposes of our department are (1) Global vision (2) Foreign language ability (3) Diversify Cultures (4) Residents of Worldwide. Excluding cultivating experts in theoretical, practical, and foreign language abilities, we're looking forward to students flourishing in this diversified department with half of people from all over 45 different countries. Under a free, diversify, open international environment, learning to become a part of this global village, and devote to international affairs.

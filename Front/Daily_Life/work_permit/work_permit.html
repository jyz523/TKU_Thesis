TKU Survival 101 ARC NHI Work Permit

Reminder

International students who wish to work in Taiwan are required to apply for a work permit before working. Please note that if you hold a job without applying for a work permit, you may be fined NTD 30,000 to NTD 150,000 and requested to leave the country immediately!

Work Permit Validity: Maximum of six months only.
Those with valid permits can only work during the school semester and cannot work after graduation or during the summer break after graduation.

Before Applying Online

  1. Get payment slip at TK001, and pay NTD100 at Post Office.
  2. Copy your student ID (front and back), and get the stamp from A Building.

Required Documents

  • Copy of student ID (with Stamp) [PDF]
  • Passport [PDF]
  • Payment receipt

Click "Apply for an account" and read the relevant regulations and laws.

  • The account number must contain both English letters and numbers.
  • The password must be at least 8 digits long and must contain English letters, numbers, and special symbols.
  • The part of identity:
    • For bachelor degree students, please select Overseas Chinese/Foreign students 4-year universities.
    • For master degree students, please select Overseas Chinese/Foreign Students - Master.

Steps to Complete Your Application

  1. After registration, please enter your account and password again to log in.
  2. After logging in, please confirm whether the login name at the top of the page is correct, and confirm whether to "Agree to accept electronic official documents":
    • "Agree": After the review is completed, an email will be sent, and the electronic official document will be available for download. You must collect it within 8 days. If not downloaded in time, the system will switch to mailing the document to the school.
    • "Disagree": After the review is completed, the paper document will be mailed to the school.

Submitting Your Application

Once logged in, navigate to "New Application and Management" and click "Add Application".

Application Process Overview

The application process is divided into six steps. Please pay attention to these six steps:

Step 1: Personal Information

Enter your personal information accurately:

Click "Next Step" to proceed to the next step after saving your application.

Step 2: School Information

Provide the required school information:

  • Faculty: Input your department.
  • School Address: Fill in "251 新北市淡水區英專路151號 (No.151, Yingzhuan Rd.)".
  • Expected Study: 4-year university students should fill "4 years"; master degree students should fill "2 years".

Click "Next Step" to continue.

Step 3: Application Form of Work Permit Information

  • Application type: Please select the work permit. If the work permit is lost and you want to reissue, please choose the reissue permit.
  • Way of receiving the official document:
    1. Electronic official document: An email will be sent to inform you whether your work permit is either approved or rejected. You must download the official document within 8 days. If not, the Ministry will switch to delivering a paper document to the school.
    2. Delivery (to the school): After the review is completed, the paper documents will be mailed to the school.
    3. Pick up in person: Pick it up at the picking counter of the Labor Development Department of the Ministry of Labor.
  • View your work permit on mobile devices: If "Yes," enter your mobile phone number and use the "Mobile Device View" function to display work permit data after verification. If not, select "No."

Step 4: Upload File

Please upload scanned PDFs of the following:

  • (1) Passport
  • (2) Student ID (front and back) with a stamp from A Building

After selecting the required files, click "Next Step" to continue.

Step 5: Examination Fee Information

Payment by post office:Take the postal transfer slip to the post office for payment first.Fill in the form according to the information on the receipt.

Use the following example format:

  • Transaction Date: Enter the date in ROC format (e.g., 107/03/21).
  • Post Office Code: Enter the provided post office code (e.g., 244128).
  • Receipt Number: Enter only the last 7 digits (e.g., 0001149).
  • Examination Fee: Input the amount (e.g., 100).

Step 6: Application Review

Please double-check that all the information is filled in correctly and uploaded, then click "Send to school for review" to complete the application.

7. Check Work Permit Arrival

After 7 days of application or once you receive the mail, scan the QR Code below to check the arrival date of your work permit.

8. Important Notes

The application materials can only be temporarily stored for seven days before being sent to the school for review. If they are not sent within this period, the system will delete your application materials.

If you encounter any problems in the operation of the system, report them immediately and ask for assistance. If the time limit for the work permit application is delayed, you will be responsible for any resulting issues.

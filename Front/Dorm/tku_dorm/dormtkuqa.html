TKU Dorm

Bed Application

  • Student ID Error: If the student ID is incorrect after logging in with your ID number, please contact the office for system administrator assistance.
    • Sung-Tao Hall contact: 02-2621-5656 ext. 2154.
    • TKU Dorm contact: 02-2626-6911 ext. 0214, 0216, or 0218.
  • Difference between Sung-Tao Hall and TKU Dorm:
    • Sung-Tao Hall is an on-campus dormitory for female students.
    • TKU Dorm is an off-campus student dormitory.
    • More details on facilities, fees, and applications can be found on the "New Student Accommodation" webpage.
  • Can I apply for a lower bunk due to health conditions?
    • Yes, but please provide relevant documentation (e.g., medical certificate) when applying for dormitory accommodation.
  • How are dorm beds allocated to freshmen?
    • For students accepted through various admissions pathways (e.g., recommendation, entrance exams), the allocation is based on residence distance from the Tamsui campus, as determined by official records.
    • If bed spaces for a certain county are insufficient, a random lottery system will determine the final list.
    • No changes to registered addresses are allowed during the application period, and address changes must meet a six-month period with at least one parent in the same household.
  • Missed the bed application period—any other chances?
    • Students who missed the initial application period, were not selected in the lottery, or could not secure a bed space in their category may apply for the waiting list.

Is it hard to find off-campus housing if I don’t get a dorm room?

  • If you do not secure a dorm room, you can refer to the Military Education Office's "Online Rental Housing Network" for relevant rental information.

Dormitory Facilities

  • Does the dorm provide bedding? The dormitory rooms are furnished with a bed frame, desk, chair (including a desk lamp), bookshelf, and wardrobe. Personal bedding is not provided and must be purchased separately. (Internet cables and locks also need to be purchased individually.)
  • Is there wireless internet in the dormitory?
    • Sung-Tao Hall offers Chunghwa Telecom wired internet, with Wi-Fi available in certain common areas.
    • TKU Dorm has fiber-optic internet, with Wi-Fi available only on even-numbered floors and the rooftop (tku_dorm_public).

Bed Size

  • Sung-Tao Hall Halls 1, 2, 3 standard rooms: Four-person rooms: 198cm x 99cm, two-person rooms: 187cm x 104cm.
  • Sung-Tao Hall Halls 4, 5 suite rooms: 200cm x 100cm.
  • TKU Dorm beds: Standard size 192cm x 90cm (large beds are 215cm x 90cm).

Entrance Control

  • Sung-Tao Hall Halls 1, 2, 3: 24-hour access with one card per person (student ID), with entrance surveillance and night security.
  • Sung-Tao Hall Halls 4, 5: Same as above.
  • TKU Dorm: 24-hour access with one card per person, professional security monitoring, and individual room keys for entry. Entry records between 24:00-06:00 are published for family reference.

Can I bring appliances into the room?

  • No. Unauthorized high-power appliances such as rice cookers, toasters, induction cookers, electric kettles, heaters, refrigerators, and TVs are prohibited in the dormitory.
  • Using two or more hair dryers at once is discouraged to prevent power outages. A dehumidifier can be borrowed upon request. For personal dehumidifiers, a special application must be submitted following the dormitory’s guidelines.

Accessible Facilities

  • Sung-Tao Hall has one 7-person accessible room (Z2201). Students with specific needs can apply for this room.
  • Other public bathrooms and suite bathrooms do not offer accessible facilities.

Public Refrigerator Management

  • Sung-Tao Hall Halls 1, 2, 3: 17 public refrigerators in the common areas.
  • Sung-Tao Hall Halls 4, 5: Each unit has its own refrigerator.
  • TKU Dorm: 12 public refrigerators, located in common areas on even-numbered floors.
  • Usage guidelines: Food storage is limited to four days, with clearly labeled items indicating room number, name, and storage date. Non-compliant items are discarded during weekly cleaning.

Is there a laundry and drying area?

  • Sung-Tao Hall 1: Laundry and drying area on the balcony behind each bathroom.
  • Sung-Tao Halls 2, 3: Laundry and drying area on the 1st floor and rooftop.
  • Sung-Tao Hall 4: Each unit has its own laundry and drying area.
  • Sung-Tao Hall 5: Laundry area on the 1st floor and drying area on the rooftop.
  • TKU Dorm: Laundry and drying area on the rooftop, with separate sections for men and women.
